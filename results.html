Mid-Size Creative Agency

Advertising & Media

Challenge

Fragmented AI experimentation across 12 teams with no consistency, shared practices, or organizational alignment. Each team was building in isolation — repeating mistakes and missing opportunities to learn from each other.

Approach

We started with a comprehensive workflow audit to identify shared pain points and high-impact opportunities. Then we deployed a 4-phase engagement: foundation training across all teams, role-specific workshops, hands-on deployment with live coaching, and Cosmo platform adoption to create a shared prompt library.

Outcome

in daily AI usage across the organization

Organization-wide fluency

Teams speak the same language about AI and workflows

Shared asset library

50+ production prompts built and maintained collaboratively

Enterprise Marketing Organization

Technology

Challenge

Leadership wanted ROI proof and stakeholder confidence, but the marketing and operations teams lacked the training infrastructure, standards, and frameworks to use AI effectively. Tools existed but adoption was spotty and results unmeasurable.

Then we deployed targeted training paired with metrics dashboards, champion programs, and Cosmo to track adoption, quality, and time savings in real time. Every adoption milestone fed stakeholder confidence.

Independent Agency Network

Professional Services

Challenge

AI anxiety was widespread among experienced staff who feared replacement or obsolescence. Resistance was high even though the organization had already invested in tooling. Without addressing the human side, no adoption would stick.

Approach

We led with empathy. We ran human-first workshops focused on empowerment, not replacement — showing how AI could elevate their work, not end it. We prioritized quick wins, built a champion program of respected internal advocates, and created a safe space for experimentation and learning.

Outcome
