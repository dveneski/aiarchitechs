Our Story

We believe in people, not just technology

Sam Klein's background in agency, media, and operator roles gave him a unique vantage point. He saw the gap: most Ai consultancies overcomplicate things or disappear after installation. Ai Architechs was founded to be the human-first alternative - practical, reliable, and genuinely invested in your success.

What We Stand Against

The Cult of Complexity

Those who worship sophistication over understanding - overcomplicating what should empower.

The industry is drowning in hype. Overcomplicated tools. Consultancies that sell fear and disappear. The Ai space has become a Cult of Complexity - a belief that sophistication equals value. We reject that entirely. Ai Architechs is the antidote: approachable, practical, and honest about what Ai can and can't do.

We are Humanists of the Digital Age - people who believe technology's highest purpose is to make work, and life, more human.

Leadership

Meet the team

SK

Sam Klein

Founder, CEO

Sam began his career on the agency side as Global Account Lead at Goodby, Silverstein & Partners, leading HP and Yahoo campaigns during the agency's back-to-back Agency of the Year years. He went on to hold senior media roles at Federated Media and spent six years as Partner at nativ.ly, a digital media company that connected brands to emerging platforms through native advertising. More recently Sam was Director of Client Partnerships at Robinhood, where he helped launch Sherwood News, advised Intuit on the rollout of SMB MediaLabs, and spent five years as Co-Founder and CEO of PAKT, a venture-backed insurance technology startup. That combination of agency, media, and operator experience is the lens Sam brings to Ai adoption: practical, execution-driven, and informed by how creative and revenue teams actually work under deadline. Sam is the architect of Cosmo and leads AiArchitechs' client engagements directly.

DV

David Veneski

Co-Founder, CMO

David spent more than two decades at Intel leading marketing, media and advertising at global scale, managing agency relationships and marketing partnerships across Intel's consumer and enterprise businesses. Most recently he led marketing at Tektronix before co-founding AiArchitechs. David has operated on the client side of agency work for longer than most people have been in the industry, which gives him a specific kind of fluency in how creative organizations actually run. He co-leads client engagements with Sam and is directly involved in all client proposal design and delivery.

MK

Max Kirchoff

Co-Founder, CTO

Max began his career on the creative side as a Creative Team Lead at Entercom and a web developer for agencies and creative studios in Portland, before moving into engineering and executive leadership across fraud detection, healthcare Ai, and data architecture. At CertifID, a wire fraud prevention platform, he held VP-level roles in Technology and Data & Trust/Risk and later worked as Principal Engineer on Ai - building privacy-preserving LLM workflows and a telemetry-driven monitoring system that prevented over 100 fraud cases and cut exposure by 60%. He was previously founding engineer and CTO at Ginko and writes Human of the Loop, a Substack on human-machine collaboration. That blend of agency-side creative roots and enterprise-grade engineering shapes Cosmo: multi-model routing, live prompt scoring, prompt library, and admin analytics - all built to enterprise standards for data handling and privacy.
